Who We Are

Vitea Studios is a Roblox development group built around creating, scaling, and maintaining modded experiences. With a growing audience of over 900,000 members, our focus is on long-term project success, consistent updates, and building games that retain players over time rather than chasing short-term trends. Looking ahead, our team is planning to expand into original, non-modded games. These projects require significant time and resources, so we're taking a slow and steady approach. We're also exploring a future project related to cybersecurity, a field we're passionate about and eager to develop further.
