Who We Are?

Vitea Studios is a Roblox development group built around creating, scaling, and maintaining modded experiences. With a growing audience of over 800,000 members, our focus is on long-term project success, consistent updates, and building games that retain players over time rather than short-term trends. Our team is planning in the feauture to expand to real and original games rather than modded ones, but those projects require a lot of time and resources, so we are taking it slow and steady. We are also planning in the future to create a project related to cybersecurity, which is a field we are very interested in and want to explore more.
