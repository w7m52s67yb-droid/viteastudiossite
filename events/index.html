Rules

Competition rules

Keep it fair and fun for everyone.

  • You must be a member of the Vitea Studios Discord server before the event ends.
  • Spam, bot messages, or repeated one-word replies will be disqualified and removed from the leaderboard.
  • All Discord server rules apply during the competition — violations can result in disqualification.
  • Prizes are distributed as Robux via the Roblox platform. You must have a valid Roblox account to claim.
  • Vitea Studios reserves the right to disqualify any participant found exploiting the leaderboard system.
  • Winners have 7 days after the event ends to claim their prize by opening a ticket in the server.
